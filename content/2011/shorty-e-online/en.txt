`Shorty <http://evo9.it>`_, a project developed by a student from the University
of Perugia during his internship here at Evonove is finally online.
It's basically an URL shortner with some enhancing features released with a
BSD license. For more details, get it on
`bitbucket <http://forge.evonove.it/shorty/overview>`_.
